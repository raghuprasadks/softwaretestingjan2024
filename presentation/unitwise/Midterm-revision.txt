Life Cycle of a bug

1. Tester identifies the bug
2. Reported to development team
3. Assign to a team member
4. Verifies whether it is a bug or not
5. Fix the bug
5. Assign back to testing team
6. Verify the fixes
7. Close the bug / reopen the bug

https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/bug-life-cycle-in-software-development/
